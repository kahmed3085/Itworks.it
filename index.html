Got Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Honest answers — no jargon, no sales spin.

How is ItWorks different from a regular IT company?
+
Traditional IT companies keep your systems running. We use AI to actively grow your business. We don't just fix problems — we eliminate them before they happen, automate the work that slows you down, and build intelligence into every layer of your operations. We're a growth partner, not a helpdesk.
What size businesses do you work with?
+
Primarily SMEs and fast-growing businesses — typically 5 to 200 employees. We find this is the sweet spot where AI delivers the fastest ROI. Smaller businesses can implement decisively, without the bureaucracy of a large enterprise. You'll see results within weeks, not quarters.
How quickly will we see results?
+
Most clients see working automations within 4–6 weeks of kickoff. Phase 1 (foundation and first automations) takes 4 weeks. By week 10 you'll have the full automation suite running. The compounding benefits — repeat customers, time savings, cost reductions — build over the following months as the AI learns your business.
Do you replace our existing IT team?
+
No — we work alongside your existing team. We handle the AI strategy, automation builds, and ongoing optimisation. If you have in-house IT staff, we free them from repetitive tasks so they can focus on higher-value work. If you don't have a tech team, we become that function for you.
What does the Discovery Call actually involve?
+
It's a 30-minute conversation — not a pitch. We ask about your business, your biggest bottlenecks, and your growth goals. By the end, you'll have a clear picture of where AI can deliver the most value for you specifically. You'll leave with honest recommendations regardless of whether we work together.
How much does it cost?
+
We don't publish fixed prices intentionally — every business has different challenges, team sizes, and goals. A solution that's right for one client would be overkill or insufficient for another. After your Discovery Call, we'll give you a clear, itemised proposal. No hidden fees, no lock-ins.
Can you work with our existing software and tools?
+
Almost certainly yes. We build integrations across CRMs, ERPs, booking systems, communication platforms, accounting tools, and custom databases. We use n8n, Make, and custom API pipelines to connect virtually any combination of tools. We work with what you have — we don't force migrations.
Is our data safe?
+
Yes. We take data security seriously on every engagement. All systems we build follow industry-standard security practices. We use encrypted connections, access-controlled environments, and handle data in line with GDPR requirements. Your data never leaves your control without explicit authorisation.
