What Enigma Does

Every Feature a Top
Real Estate Agent Needs

Enigma handles the time-consuming parts of real estate so your team can focus on building relationships and closing.

🎯

Intelligent Lead Qualification

Automatically qualifies inbound leads by budget, timeline, location preference, and property type. Only warm leads reach your agents.

📅

Automated Viewing Scheduler

Enigma books property viewings directly into your calendar — handling availability, confirmations, and reminders without any manual input.

🏠

Smart Property Matching

Analyses prospect preferences and instantly matches them to available listings — sending personalised property packs with AI-generated summaries.

🔄

Automated Follow-Up Sequences

Multi-step follow-up cadences triggered by prospect behaviour — nudging cold leads back to warm, then warm to booked, fully on autopilot.

📊

Market Intelligence Reports

Sends prospects AI-generated local market reports — average prices, trends, days on market — positioning your agency as the expert before the meeting.

⭐

Review & Reputation Automation

After every completed sale or letting, Enigma automatically requests a Google review — growing your agency's reputation without lifting a finger.
