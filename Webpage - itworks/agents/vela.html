What Vela Does

The Customer Success AI That
Never Misses a Signal

Vela watches every account so you don't have to — and acts on what it finds before a problem becomes a cancellation.

🔮

Predictive Churn Scoring

Vela scores every account daily based on 40+ health signals — usage drop, support tickets, login frequency, and NPS — flagging at-risk accounts weeks before they churn.

📣

Proactive Intervention Playbooks

When Vela detects a health score drop, it automatically fires the right playbook: a personalised check-in email, a Slack alert to your CSM, or a tailored success plan.

📈

Expansion Revenue Detection

Identifies accounts that are ready to upgrade based on usage patterns and business growth signals — and prompts your team to reach out at exactly the right moment.

🎯

Quarterly Business Reviews on Autopilot

Vela generates tailored QBR decks for each account — pulling in their usage data, ROI metrics, and success milestones — ready to present in minutes, not hours.

Customer-Facing Chat Support

Deep CRM & Product Integration
